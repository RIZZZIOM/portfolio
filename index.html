about

I'm an offensive security professional specializing in red and purple team operations for enterprise environments, with experience across web, network, and cloud infrastructure. Alongside client work, I build custom offensive tooling and automation in Python and Go, and dig into independent vulnerability research.

I work alongside blue teams to validate detection and close gaps, and share what comes out of my independent research and personal experience through exploit write-ups, CTF challenge solutions, conference talks, blogs, and open-source tooling. Continuous learning is core to how I work — right now I'm going deep on AI engineering to architect smarter security tools rather than just use them.
